The Basic HTML Basics Website


Introduction to basic HTML syntax

It's all about the tags! Learn the tags, add a little UI/UX pixie dust and you'll be well on your way. Just make sure you have someone you can get to critique, or leave feedback before it hits the portfolio.

The Must-Know Tags:

Heading Tags:

<h1> - <h6>   This will result in text being written with a heading format. The size of the heading will depend on the number chosen (1-6).

Paragraph Tags:

<p>   This formats the text to be written without emphasis or bolding to represent a paragraph's attributes. These are the main tags used for the text content of your site.

Body Tags:

<body>   These tags represent the main body of the website- that is where most of the content is located. Most <h1> and <p> tags are located with the body tags.
